Who is Athena Asamiya ? She is a woman fighter into the great tournament King of Fighters (created by SNK). She's a main character since the first tournament in 1994 to 2003.

Her martial art is composed by chinese Kung Fu and her psycho power which come from her origin as the greek godess Athena and follow the same way of life : "fight to protect the world against the Evil".

She's my favourite character because I like her gameplay, her voice and her look.
Here, this is the 2002 version of her, surely one of the best version by SNK PLAYMORE.

It's a quick presentation but this maybe make you read the other pages.
